Print Media Advertising



Print Media holds a vital significance even today in the modern digital world. The glory of print media advertising will never die regardless of how convenient the digital model of the advertisement has become today. The print media can help brands make a strong footprint in the minds of the consumers.

Odd Balls expertise and experience strives to use the various forms of print media advertisement to build a strong corporate image for your brand. With a judicious use if layout, colors and creative ideas, the content of the print media shall surely appeal to the readers. We offer the following print media advertising services:

Brochure design

Our brochure designs will surely add great positive value to your brand and ensure that it stays relevant and visible in the target consumers mind.

Business collateral

The business collateral includes media and materials used to create awareness of your brand and promote it through effective marketing strategies. The collateral materials are the first impression that your cam makes on your consumers. Odd Balls execute the best collateral designs and materials to enhance the value of your brand and its offerings to get the right message through to the audience.

Signage’s

Signages are the perfect way to promote your brand by engaging your audience most effectively and get the right message through. Trust Odd Balls for dynamically creating signages which are engaging and innovative to immediately attract the attention of the target audience.

Banners

Odd Balls focus on creating customized banners with innovative infographics to gain the attention of the audience.

Newspaper publication

The newspaper is the most popular and universal tool to reach out to a large audience. We tie-up with the top-trending newspaper publications to make your business popular.

Magazine advertising

The elegant and sophisticated designer print advertising in popular magazines is a great way to establish a connection with the target audience.

Flyers, pamphlets, and posters

Flyers, pamphlets, and posters are a great way to reach out to get the brand message across to the audience. Odd Balls create and delivers impressive, innovative and eye-catchy pamphlets and flyers to help you stay on top of the mind of the audience.
